About Me

I’m Hamza Mohsin, a dedicated UX Designer and Researcher with 5 years of experience in front-end development. I specialize in creating user-centric designs through meticulous research and innovative solutions. My work spans design, research, and development, ensuring holistic and effective user experiences. Let’s connect to explore how we can create impactful designs together.

Front End Developer

With over four years of experience as a front-end developer, I specialize in crafting responsive, user-friendly interfaces using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. I have worked across multiple industries, honing my skills in web performance optimization and cross-browser compatibility. My focus is on delivering high-quality code and seamless user experiences across diverse development environments.

UI/UX Designer

I'm currently pursuing a Master’s in User Experience Design, working on diverse projects that involve wireframing, prototyping, and user research. My focus is on crafting intuitive, user-centered designs that meet user needs and expectations.

Collaborator

I excel in collaborative environments, effectively working with frontend and backend developers, designers, and stakeholders. My strong communication and teamwork skills ensure smooth project progression and successful outcomes.

A Diverse Upbringing

I was born and raised in Pakistan, moving across various states throughout my childhood. This diverse upbringing instilled in me the ability to adapt quickly to new challenges and environments, a skill that has proven invaluable in both my personal and professional life. I have always embraced the cultural richness around me, allowing these influences to seep into my design ethos. I aim to create designs that are not only user-friendly but also culturally inclusive and empathetic to diverse audiences.

Professional Journey

My professional journey started in 2018 at Techverx as an intern. Over four years, I advanced to Senior Software Engineer, honing my skills in translating design concepts into functional, user-friendly code. This experience fueled my passion for creating intuitive digital products and reinforced my commitment to human-centered design. I believe technology should simplify and enhance users’ lives, not complicate them.

A New Chapter: UX Design

This passion led me to pivot my career towards user experience design, a field where I could marry my technical skills with my love for human-centered design. I am currently pursuing a Master’s degree in UX Design at Technische Hochschule Ingolstadt. Here, I have the opportunity to dive deeper into the design process, explore innovative solutions, and challenge myself to think critically about how to improve user interactions.

Looking Forward

As I continue my journey in UX design, I am driven by the desire to create products that resonate with users on a deeper level. My goal is to design with empathy, understanding, and innovation, ensuring that every digital interaction is a positive and enriching experience. I believe that technology, when designed thoughtfully, has the power to transform lives and connect people across the globe.
