Master’s Thesis HCI Research · 2025–2026

UAV Dispatcher Interface

Designing a UAV-based information retrieval interface to support firefighting dispatchers during multitasking and interruptions.

psychology

Research Focus

Emergency dispatchers operate under high workload, frequent task switching, and constant interruptions, which significantly degrade situational awareness.

groups

Participants

N = 29

Students, volunteers, and professional dispatchers

flight

System Context

  • • Dispatchers manage emergency calls and resource allocation
  • • UAVs provide early aerial situational awareness
  • • Existing UAV interfaces target field responders, not dispatchers
