Design Reflection

Fog Interface — Challenges & Decisions

Challenge

Fog removes depth perception. Overly precise visuals risk distraction in already stressful conditions.

Design Decision

Abstract distance bars and a vehicle-centric icon were used instead of numeric distances to reduce cognitive load.

Evaluation Insight

Non-significant results reflected limitations of sample size and lab realism rather than interface irrelevance.

Key Takeaways

  • Abstraction is critical in degraded perception environments
  • Design value can exist without statistical significance
  • Future work requires real-world driving contexts
