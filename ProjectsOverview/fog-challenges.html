Design Reflection

Fog Interface — Challenges & Decisions

Challenge

Fog eliminates reliable depth cues. Overly precise or dense visualizations risk increasing cognitive load and distracting drivers in already stressful situations.

Design Decision

Replace numeric distance values with abstract distance bars and a vehicle-centric icon to communicate proximity intuitively while minimizing cognitive effort.

Evaluation Insight

Non-significant statistics mainly reflected sample size and lab realism limitations, rather than lack of interface usefulness.

Key Takeaways

  • • Abstraction is essential in degraded perception environments
  • • Design value can exist beyond statistical significance
  • • Real-world driving contexts are critical for future validation
