Project 3 · Automotive HCI

Fog Interface — Results

Main Finding

Visual distance cues showed behavioral trends toward faster reactions, but differences between conditions were not statistically significant (t-test = 0.16).

With Visual Distance Cues

Abstract bars and a vehicle-centric icon conveyed relative distance without numeric precision.

Without Visual Cues

Baseline condition relying solely on degraded natural visibility in fog.

Interpretation

Although statistical significance was not reached, the results provide design insights into how abstraction can support perception under degraded visual conditions.
