Design Reflection

UAV — Challenges & Design Decisions

Key constraints encountered during the UAV dispatcher study and the design decisions made to address them.

warning
CHALLENGE

Information Overload

Continuous UAV video, event streams, and telemetry increased cognitive demand, particularly when dispatchers were interrupted and had to re-engage with an ongoing incident.

tune
DESIGN DECISION

Structured Re-engagement Support

Instead of relying on continuous video monitoring, the interface prioritized structured elements such as event logs, timelines, filters, and concise re-engagement summaries.

Chosen over alert-only or raw-video approaches to reduce cognitive load during interruption recovery.

visibility
EVALUATION INSIGHT

Attention Allocation

Descriptive eye-tracking observations and participant feedback indicated greater visual focus on structured summaries and timelines, while continuous video feeds were consulted selectively rather than continuously.

forward Limitations & Next Steps

  • Participant Sample

    Predominantly students; limited number of professional dispatchers.

  • Short-Term Exposure

    Single-session evaluation limits insights into learning and trust.

  • Operational Scope

    Future work should include additional incident types and live dispatch environments.
