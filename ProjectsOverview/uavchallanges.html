Analysis & Reflection

UAV — Challenges & Future Work

Key challenges identified during the design and evaluation of the UAV information retrieval interface, followed by directions for future research.

warning
CHALLENGE

Information Overload

Dispatchers were exposed to continuous high-density UAV telemetry, increasing cognitive demand during multitasking and interruptions.

filter_list
DESIGN RESPONSE

Structured Summaries

Timeline views, filtering, and re-engagement summaries were introduced to support rapid reconstruction of incident state after interruptions.

visibility
OBSERVATION

Visual Attention Patterns

Descriptive eye-tracking observations indicated that participants focused more on structured interface elements (e.g., timeline and summaries) rather than continuous video feeds.

science Future Work

  • Professional Dispatchers

    Evaluation with experienced dispatchers in real emergency control centers.

  • Longitudinal Use

    Studying learning effects, trust, and sustained use over extended periods.

  • Broader Scenarios

    Extension to additional incident types and operational contexts.
