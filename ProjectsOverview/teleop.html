Project 2 Remote Vehicle Teleoperation

Teleoperation Audio Feedback

Investigating how audio, audio-to-visual, and audio + visual feedback influence remote vehicle takeover decisions.

warning

Why this matters

In teleoperation, operators are physically separated from the vehicle and must rely entirely on mediated sensory feedback. During emergency or safety-critical situations, delayed or overloaded feedback can directly affect decision quality.

tune

What we compared

  • volume_up Audio-only cues (e.g., sirens, approaching vehicles)
  • visibility Audio-to-visual conversion using on-screen alerts
  • dashboard Combined audio + visual feedback during takeover

Participants

N = 15

Design

Between-group

Scenarios

4 driving situations

Measures

RT · Errors · SART

Research focus

How do different feedback modalities influence teleoperator performance (RQ1) and situational awareness (RQ2) during remote vehicle takeover?
