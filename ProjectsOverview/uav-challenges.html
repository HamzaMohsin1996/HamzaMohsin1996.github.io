UAV — Challenges & Decisions

Key constraints encountered during the design and evaluation of the UAV-based dispatcher interface, and the rationale behind critical design and methodological decisions.

warning

Key Challenges

  • • Dispatchers operate under frequent interruptions, requiring rapid re-engagement with incident information.
  • • Continuous UAV video streams introduced information overload rather than improving situational awareness.
  • • Re-engagement after task switching was not supported by existing UAV interfaces.
  • • Measuring situational awareness without disrupting time-critical workflows posed methodological constraints.
  • • Real emergency control centers could not be used due to ethical, safety, and availability limitations.
engineering

Design & Methodological Decisions

  • • Introduced structured summaries, logs, and timelines to support rapid re-engagement after interruptions.
  • • Prioritized abstracted information over raw video to reduce cognitive load.
  • • Adopted a within-subjects experimental design to control for individual differences.
  • • Selected SPAM instead of freeze-based methods (e.g., SAGAT) to avoid interrupting dispatcher workflows.
  • • Used a high-fidelity simulation environment to balance experimental control with ecological validity.
