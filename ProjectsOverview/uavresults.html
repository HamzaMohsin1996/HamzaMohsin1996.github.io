chat Qualitative Observations (Post-Study Interviews)

Structured re-engagement after interruptions

“You just have everything at a glance what happened even if you were busy with something else.” (Expert 1)

Reduced reliance on continuous video monitoring

“It’s better if the information is on the page, and not only in the video.” (Expert 2)

Information richness increases early effort

“There was so much to see that I didn’t even notice the information on the left side at first.” (Expert 1)

Filtering and visual cues support prioritization

“If the information you missed is highlighted with a different colour, you immediately know something new happened.” (Expert 2)
