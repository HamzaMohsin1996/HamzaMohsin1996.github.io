Project 3 · Automotive HCI

Fog Distance Perception Interface

Supporting driver distance perception in foggy conditions using abstract visual cues when natural visibility breaks down.

Problem

Fog removes reliable depth cues. Existing vehicle interfaces do not compensate, increasing accident risk in low visibility.

Research Question

How can visual distance cues in automotive interfaces improve driver understanding of surrounding vehicles in fog?

Study Approach

Controlled lab experiment comparing driving with and without abstract visual distance cues in simulated fog scenarios.

Participants

N = 18

Design

Between-group

Scenarios

2 emergency cases

Measures

RT, Errors, UEQ
