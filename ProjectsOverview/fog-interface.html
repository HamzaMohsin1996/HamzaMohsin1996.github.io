Project 3 · Automotive HCI

Fog Distance Perception Interface

Supporting driver distance perception in foggy conditions using abstract visual cues when natural visibility breaks down.

Problem

Fog removes reliable depth cues. Existing vehicle interfaces fail to compensate, increasing accident risk in low-visibility environments.

Research Question

How can visual distance cues improve driver understanding of surrounding vehicles under fog conditions?

Study Approach

Controlled laboratory experiment comparing driving with and without abstract visual cues in simulated fog.
